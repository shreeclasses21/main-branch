Privacy Policy

At Shree Classes, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email, contact number, location, and usage behavior when you register, access lessons, or interact with our platform.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide educational services, respond to inquiries, send updates, improve the platform, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Security

We use secure servers, encryption, and access control measures to protect your personal information. No method of transmission is 100% secure, but we strive to ensure industry-standard practices.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. It may only be shared with trusted service providers for internal use or where legally required.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies to track usage and personalize your experience. You can manage cookie settings in your browser. Our analytics tools help us improve user experience.

6. User Rights

You can request access to your data, corrections, or deletion by contacting us at shreeclasses21@gmail.com.

7. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or prominent notice on our platform.
